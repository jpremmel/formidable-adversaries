Formidable Adversaries

Carol the
Cactus

Carol the Cactus

Carol used to work at a bookstore before joining a fight club and leaving work life behind her. She has become the leader of her local fight chapter and is a force to be reckoned with.

Fight Skills:

  • Spike Darts with Poison
  • Flash Flood Drown
  • Harness Power of Sun to Blind Opponent

Octavia the
Octopus

Octavia the Octopus

Eight times the destruction. Eight times the pain. Don't make Octavia mad or she will slap you eight times over and blind you with a debilitating ink spray.

Fight Skills:

  • Ink Spray
  • Camoflauge and Attack
  • Venomous Beak Bite

Bruce the
Baboon

Bruce the Baboon

Mighty Bruce is the king of his own jungle. Arguably the fiercest animal to ever come out of nature, Bruce will crush your hopes, dreams, and your will to live with his ear-shattering banshee cry.

Fight Skills:

  • Shattering Banshee Cry
  • Power Bite
  • Tail Chokeout

Lenny
Crabitz

Lenny Crabitz

Raised on the beaches of Los Angeles, Lenny's mother was a crab who was recently served a restraining order for stalking hit musical sensation/style icon Lenny Kravitz.

Fight Skills:

  • Death Pinch
  • Scarf Strangle
  • Silent but Deadly Fart

Pterry the
Pterodactyl

Pterry the Pterodactyl

A nice guy who always wanted to help others, Pterry eventually got tired of everyone being afraid of him just because of how he looks. He finally decided to give them a reason to fear him by using his sonic boom wing flap.

Fight Skills:

  • Eye Gouge
  • Swoop & Dive Attack
  • Sonic Boom Wing Flap

Patti the
Penguin

Patti the Penguin

Patti may be the cutest fighter of the bunch, but don't fall victim to her adorable belly. She was abandoned as a young pup and adopted by assassins who taught her how to be the fiercest icicle-thrower this side of the Mississippi.

Fight Skills:

  • Flap Slap
  • Belly Slide & Attack
  • Icicle Spear

Misty the
Mountain Goat

Misty the Mountain Goat

Bored from her mountain life of chewing weeds and kicking rocks, Misty wandered down to the plains one day and was snatched up by a pair of evil poachers. Her extreme fear caused her to harness her hitherto latent super powers. Ultimately, she head-butted her poachers so hard that no one ever heard from them again.

Fight Skills:

  • Head Butt
  • Hind leg Powerkick
  • BAAttle Cry

Gart
the Gator

Gart the Gator

Gart looks like a nice guy, but he's only nice when he's not hungry. He's hungry about 99% of his waking life, so watch out! He's got a gigantic mouth and will not hesitate to swallow you whole.

Fight Skills:

  • Snapper Bite
  • Death Roll - Bite & Drown
  • Tail Smack

Paco
the Pig

Paco the Pig

Paco doesn't even want to try to seem nice. He ate too much, he's sleepy, he's cranky, and he wants to smother-sit on you just so you will stop talking.

Fight Skills:

  • Oink to Death
  • Smother Sit
  • Throw Acidic Mud
